Our Story

More than just a cleaning service, we're a team dedicated to bringing peace of mind and order to Cape Town's homes and businesses.

25 Years of Trusted Service

Founded on the principle of providing reliable, high-quality service, Nieuwburg has grown from a small team into one of the city's most respected cleaning and placement agencies. We believe that a clean environment is the foundation for a productive and peaceful life, and we bring that philosophy to every job, big or small.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide the help you need, without the hassle. This means supervised, fully-equipped teams who arrive on time, respect your space, and deliver a consistent, high-quality clean every single time. It's not just a clean—it's the Nieuwburg standard.
